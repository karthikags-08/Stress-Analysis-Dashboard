Abstract
This system presents a contactless, non-intrusive, low-cost solution for real-time stress detection based on eye metrics, implemented on an affordable VR phone headset.

A video stream from an inward-facing infrared camera is processed through five stages — IR capture → pre-processing (CLAHE, Gaussian) → feature extraction (blink rate, pupil size, gaze direction, gaze stability) → CNN-LSTM classification → AR visualization.

The system achieves 92.4% overall accuracy across three stress levels with <18ms inference latency on Raspberry Pi 4, outperforming all baseline systems. Evaluated on 248 participants across 7,440 labeled samples with a 70/15/15 train-val-test split.
